About i Salon

Redefining Beauty in Patna

At i Salon Hair & Beauty Makeup, we believe that beauty is an art and styling is a masterpiece. Located opposite Tata Guinea Motors on East Boring Canal Road, we bring international standards of grooming and styling to Patna.

Our team of professional hair stylists and certified beauty experts are dedicated to enhancing your natural beauty. Whether it's a transformative haircut, flawless bridal makeup, or rejuvenating skin care, we ensure you leave our salon feeling confident and radiant.
